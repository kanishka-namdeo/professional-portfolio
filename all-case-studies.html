Context & Challenge

Cognium was founded with a bold vision to transform wealth management through artificial intelligence. As the inaugural Senior Product Manager, I was tasked with defining and executing the product strategy for an AI-native investment platform from absolute zero.

The Core Challenge: Build a differentiated AI-powered wealth management product that could compete with established fintech players while leveraging cutting-edge LLM technology to deliver genuinely personalized investment guidance.

Market Opportunity: The wealth management industry was undergoing significant transformation, with increasing demand for personalized, accessible investment advice. Traditional advisory services remained expensive and inaccessible to the mass affluent segment, while existing robo-advisors offered limited personalization and generic recommendations.

Technical Complexity: Building an AI-native financial product required navigating significant technical challenges around model reliability, hallucination prevention, and real-time data integration. The product needed to balance AI capabilities with the precision required for financial decision-making.

Competitive Landscape: Established players like Betterment and Wealthfront dominated the robo-advisory space, while new AI-first entrants like Jarvis and others were beginning to emerge. Differentiation required a fundamentally new approach to how AI could enhance the investment experience.
