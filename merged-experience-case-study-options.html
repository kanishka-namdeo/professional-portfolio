MoveInSync: 10x ARR Growth Case Study

The Challenge

Enterprise clients struggled with fragmented commute management across multiple locations. Legacy systems couldn't scale, resulting in 35% higher operational costs and declining employee satisfaction scores. The company needed a unified solution that could handle complex enterprise requirements while maintaining a seamless user experience.

My Approach

I conducted 50+ user interviews with enterprise admins and commuters to identify pain points. Based on insights, I defined the product vision and built a unified platform with route optimization, real-time tracking, and seamless ERP integrations. I implemented A/B testing to iterate on key flows and worked closely with engineering to deliver features on time.

Results Delivered

Achieved 10x ARR growth through product-led expansion. Scaled from 20 to 70+ locations across 3 countries. Reduced client implementation time by 40% through standardized onboarding flows.
